# Rad Defence - New Tower Sound FX Pack

Format:
- Mono WAV
- 44.1 kHz
- 16-bit PCM
- Designed as short, punchy tower-defence SFX

Files:
- tower_mortar_fire.wav — low tube-thump launch
- tower_mortar_impact.wav — small explosive impact
- tower_drone_launch.wav — drone spin-up/deploy
- drone_attack_laser.wav — short roving drone attack zap
- tower_electric_fence_zap.wav — one-shot damage zap
- tower_electric_fence_loop.wav — low electric fence idle loop
- tower_electric_fence_break.wav — fence overload/destruction sound
- tower_artillery_fire.wav — heavier cannon shot
- tower_artillery_impact.wav — heavier explosion impact

Suggested usage:
- Mortar: play fire immediately, play impact at projectile landing.
- Drone tower: play launch when drone deploys, laser per attack.
- Electric fence: loop idle quietly while active, zap on enemy contact, break when destroyed.
- Artillery: fire + delayed impact, with lower fire rate than mortar.
